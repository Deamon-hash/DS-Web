Privacy Policy

At DeepSeas DMCC, safeguarding your privacy and securing your information is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we ensure confidentiality. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collection

We prioritize transparency. We do not collect any personal information when you visit our website. Our client relationships are direct, and we do not engage in list sourcing. This commitment has been built over years of trusted partnerships.

2. Sharing of Information

DeepSeas DMCC does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information entrusted to us from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. While we strive to maintain high security standards, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any significant changes will be communicated via this platform, email notifications, or our official website.
