Tanker Chartering

DeepSeas DMCC’s tanker division works closely with oil companies, traders, shipowners, and operators across global markets. Our independent platform and international reach allow us to deliver commercially focused solutions supported by real-time market intelligence and strong industry relationships.

We manage tanker chartering requirements worldwide, covering spot, time charter, and contracts of affreightment for the transportation of crude oil and refined petroleum products. Our experience spans all tanker sizes, ensuring efficient execution and reliable support across every stage of the chartering process.

LPG Shipbroking

DeepSeas DMCC provides specialized LPG shipbroking services across all vessel segments, from VLGCs to semi-refrigerated and pressurised carriers. Our team continuously monitors global markets to deliver timely intelligence covering LPG, ammonia, and petrochemical transportation.

Our Capabilities
  • Spot & Term Chartering : Global LPG Markets
  • Contracts of Affreightment : Tailored Solutions
  • Market Intelligence : LPG, Ammonia & Petrochemicals

LNG Shipbroking

DeepSeas DMCC provides LNG shipbroking services with a strong focus on commercial execution and strategic market support. We work closely with clients to identify, develop, and implement real-time LNG shipping strategies, supporting each phase of the opportunity with disciplined execution and market insight.

Our team has a deep understanding of the complex dynamics of the LNG market, combining technical knowledge with commercial expertise. This enables us to assist clients in structuring, negotiating, and executing LNG shipping solutions aligned with evolving global energy trade requirements.

Dry Cargo Brokerage

DeepSeas DMCC has a strong presence in the dry cargo market, supporting the transportation of a wide range of bulk commodities worldwide. Our experience covers key cargoes including iron ore, grain, steel, coal, cement, wood products, and other dry bulk materials.

We operate across all major vessel segments, from capesize and panamax to supramax and handysize vessels. Our team provides region-specific market intelligence and commercial support, enabling clients to make informed decisions across diverse global trading routes.

Sale & Purchase

DeepSeas DMCC’s sale and purchase division advises national and international clients on vessel acquisitions and disposals across multiple asset classes. We provide confidential, market-driven support for transactions involving tankers, bulk carriers, gas carriers, container vessels, offshore units, and car carriers.

Our experience covers both newbuilding projects and second-hand vessel sales. Demolition and recycling form a key focus area, supported by strong relationships with leading global cash buyers, ensuring efficient execution and reliable outcomes throughout the transaction process.

Commercial Management

DeepSeas DMCC’s commercial management team handles contract negotiation and vessel employment across crude tankers, product tankers, LPG, LNG, and dry cargo vessels operating in the spot market. Our focus is on efficient employment, disciplined execution, and consistent customer satisfaction.

Our Capabilities
  • Contract Negotiation & Fixing : Spot Market Employment
  • Vessel Marketing & Positioning : Global Customer Network
  • Earnings Optimization : Commercial Performance Focus
