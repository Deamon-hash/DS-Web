Our History

A team of well-experienced brokers, commercial and technical consultants serving global shipping markets.

DeepSeas DMCC has been providing clients with valuable insight into the international oil, gas, dry cargo, sale & purchase, and shipping markets. Through consistent performance, professional integrity, and market expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted and independent shipbroking firm.

Our team comprises experienced brokers, commercial specialists, and technical consultants focused on the energy and ocean transportation industries. With precise, up-to-date market intelligence, we facilitate negotiations with a strong emphasis on transparency, discretion, and ensuring that our clients receive the best possible information to support confident decision-making.
